Why Tempus

Every great idea begins with a frustration. Tempus was born out of the countless hours wasted juggling clunky spreadsheets, chasing down missing timesheets, and trying to make sense of scattered data. We asked ourselves a simple question: what if time registration could be effortless, elegant, and empowering?

Tempus is the answer.

Built with precision, crafted for clarity, and designed to delight, Tempus is more than just a timesheet tool — it’s your partner in productivity. Every feature has been shaped with care: from lightning-fast entry that feels natural, to rock-solid storage that never forgets, to clean weekly overviews that make managers cheer.

Why is Tempus so good to use? Because it works with you, not against you. No bloated menus. No confusing workflows. Just the essentials — done brilliantly. Hours and notes slide in smoothly, totals calculate themselves, and your data lives safely in your browser until you need it. Switching weeks? Instant. Reviewing the past? Crystal clear. Adding a new row? Already waiting for you.

  • Unmatched simplicity — intuitive enough that anyone can master it in seconds.
  • Unstoppable speed — because logging time should take less time than doing it.
  • Unquestionable reliability — your data stays safe, synced, and always at your fingertips.
  • Unparalleled flexibility — whether you’re tracking a single project ID or juggling dozens, Tempus adapts effortlessly.

Tempus isn’t just another time registration tool. It’s a declaration: that your time is valuable, your focus is sacred, and your workflow deserves nothing less than excellence.
